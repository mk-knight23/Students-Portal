The AME.HUB features 6 distinct entrypoints, each tailored to a specific user persona.

## 1. Student Portal (`/portal/student`)
- **Dashboard**: Track admission progress (Applied -> Documentation -> Counseling -> Allotted).
- **Documents**: Upload and verify academic records.
- **Counseling**: Preference builder for college selection.

## 2. Parent Portal (`/portal/parent`)
- **Dashboard**: High-level view of ward's progress.
- **Payments**: Detailed fee breakdown and payment links.

## 3. Office Staff Portal (`/portal/staff`)
- **Dashboard**: Region-specific KPI counter (Pending Verifications, Payments).
- **Verifications**: Review and approve student document uploads.
- **Student Master**: Manage regional student lists.

## 4. Admin Portal (`/portal/admin`)
- **Dashboard**: Global system health and executive monitors.
- **Management**: Branch-level controls and agency settings.
- **Student Master**: Full access to all student records.

## 5. Agent Portal (`/portal/agent`)
- **Referrals**: Tracker for students referred to the platform.
- **Commissions**: Real-time commission payout monitoring.

## 6. Auditor Portal (`/portal/auditor`)
- **Compliance**: DPDPA 2023 alignment monitors.
- **Audit Logs**: Read-only history of sensitive data access.
